About

Queenex Tissues Factory (QTF) is a well-established sole proprietorship company and the eldest business entity of the prestigious local conglomerate, Al Hajeri International Group, headquartered in Mussafah, Abu Dhabi.

The company has been serving the tissue paper industry since the inception in 1978 with markets in GCC, Asian and European countries. Our main business activities are manufacturing, marketing and distribution of all kinds of tissue products and grades.
